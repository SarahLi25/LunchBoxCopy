Chef Samuel Wagoner

Culinary Arts Instructor

Chef Wagoner brings years of professional culinary experience to the SICTC Culinary Arts program. Under his guidance, students learn essential kitchen skills, food safety, and the art of creating memorable dining experiences. His passion for teaching and commitment to excellence ensures every meal served at The LunchBox meets the highest standards.

Program Description

Culinary Arts I and II prepare students for occupations and higher education programs related to the entire spectrum of careers in the food industry including food production, hospitality, tourism, nutrition, and food science.

Students gain hands-on experience in food preparation, sanitation, purchasing, kitchen operations, hospitality management, and customer service through intensive lab experiences and commercial applications.
